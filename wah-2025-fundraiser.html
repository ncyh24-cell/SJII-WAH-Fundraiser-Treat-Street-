About WAH Foundation

The WAH Foundation, established in 2009, is a Cambodia-registered NGO dedicated to improving lives in rural Kampong Chhnang through clean water, healthcare, and education. With 340 water tanks installed, 400 midwives trained, and 34 women empowered through scholarships, WAH transforms communities. Learn more at wahfoundation.org.
